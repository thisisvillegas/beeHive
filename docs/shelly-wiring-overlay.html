Your Shelly Plus RGBW PM - Wiring Guide

Bedroom Light Control - Only 3 terminals needed!

Wiring Summary

Step 1: Red 18 AWG wire from BED fuse terminal
+
Step 2: Black 18 AWG wire from ground bus
⏊
Step 3: Original BED wire (to bedroom lights)
R / O1
That's it! Just 3 wires into 3 terminals:
  • + = Power in (12V from fuse)
  • ⏊ = Ground
  • R/O1 = Output to your bedroom lights
Note: The other terminals (W, B, G, I1-I4) are for:
  • W, B, G (O4, O3, O2) - Extra output channels for other lights later
  • I1-I4 - Physical switch inputs (optional - for wall switches)
You don't need these for basic bedroom light control!

Terminal Screw Tips

  1. Use a small flathead screwdriver
  2. Loosen the screw, insert stripped wire (~5mm bare)
  3. Tighten firmly - give it a gentle tug to confirm it's secure
  4. Make sure no bare copper is exposed outside the terminal
