Step-by-Step Wiring Instructions

1 Disconnect Power
Turn off the RV's main 12V disconnect switch or disconnect the house battery. Verify no power at the fuse panel with a multimeter.
2 Locate the "Bed" Fuse Output
Find the load side of the "Bed" 15A fuse. This is where 12V goes OUT to the bedroom lights. You'll tap into this wire (or the terminal it connects to).
3 Wire the Shelly Power Input
Shelly Terminal Connect To Wire Color
DC+ Bed fuse LOAD side (+12V) Red
GND Ground bus bar Black
Use a fuse tap or add-a-fuse adapter for a clean connection, or use a wire nut / Wago connector to splice into the existing wire.
4 Wire the Shelly Output
Shelly Terminal Connect To Wire Color
O1 Bedroom light + (positive) Green or White
The light's negative (-) wire stays connected to ground as it already is.
5 Power On and Configure
  1. Reconnect the 12V power
  2. Shelly should create a WiFi hotspot: ShellyPlusRGBWPM-XXXX
  3. Connect to it from your phone
  4. Go to 192.168.33.1 in a browser
  5. Configure your WiFi credentials
  6. Enable MQTT: Server = 192.168.0.95 (your Pi)

Important Notes:
  • The Shelly Plus RGBW PM is a dimmer - your LEDs will be dimmable!
  • If lights flicker, enable "High Frequency PWM" in Shelly settings
  • Use channel O1 for the bedroom, you have 3 more channels (O2-O4) for other lights
